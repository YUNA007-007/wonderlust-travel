Introduction

Imagine an island with turquoise blue waters, secret coves, soft golden sands, lush green foliage, rugged mountains, picturesque villages and a vibrant capital city. The island and its capital, Palma, are overflowing with character, while its beaches are known around the world, making Majorca one of the most sought‑after summer destinations.
